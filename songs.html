Minase Inori

Minase Inori is a voice actress and singer who has made a name for herself in both industries. She started her career in 2010 as a voice actress and has since become one of the most talented in the field. Inori's voice acting most famously feautured as Rem on "Re:Zero Starting Life in Another World," On the other hand, her music career began in 2015 with the release of her debut single "Yume no Tsubomi," and she has since many more songs. Her talent as both a voice actress and singer has earned her numerous awards and nominations, including the Best Supporting Actress Award and the Best Singing Award at the 13th and 14th Seiyu Awards.

Aqours

Aqours is a idol group formed in 2015 as part of the multimedia project Love Live! Sunshine!!. The group consists of nine members and they have gained a large following both in Japan and internationally. Aqours has released numerous singles and albums, and their music often features upbeat and catchy tunes. The group has won several awards for their music, including the Best Idol Award at the 12th Seiyu Awards and the Best Theme Song Award at the 6th Anime Trending Awards. Their performances have also been praised, and they have won the Live Performance Award at the 11th Seiyu Awards.

Nijigasaki High School Idol Club

Nijigasaki High School Idol Club is a multimedia project that features a group of fictional idols who attend Nijigasaki High School. The group initially debuted as part of the Love Live! franchise in 2017. Nijigasaki is known for their catchy and upbeat pop music, which features a mix of genres, including EDM, rock, and J-pop. The group has released several albums and singles, including "Love U my friends" and "DIVE!". Nijigasaki has won several awards, including the Best Theme Song Award at the 2021 Anime Awards for their song "Tokimeki Runners".

ClariS

Claris is a pop music duo consisting of singers Clara and Alice. They are known for their sweet and upbeat vocals that are featured in many anime series, including "Ore no Imouto ga Konna ni Kawaii Wake ga Nai" and "Eromanga Sensei." In addition to their successful music career, Claris has also won several awards, including the New Artist of the Year at the 27th Japan Gold Disc Award and the Animation Artist of the Year at the 9th CD Shop Awards. Their catchy tunes and charming performances have captured the hearts of fans around the world, making them one of the most popular anime music acts today.
