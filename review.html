Reviews

Love Live! Sunshine

Love Live Sunshine is a Japanese anime series that aired from July to September 2016. It follows the story of a group of high school girls from Uranohoshi Girls' High School who aim to become school idols in order to prevent their school from closing down. The second season of the series aired from October to December 2017, and a film titled Love Live! Sunshine!! The School Idol Movie: Over the Rainbow was released in January 2019.

9/10

Eromanga-Sensei

Eromanga Sensei is a romantic comedy anime series that follows the story of Masamune Izumi, a high school student and light novel author who goes by the pseudonym Eromanga Sensei. He works with a mysterious illustrator, who he later discovers is his reclusive younger sister Sagiri, with whom he develops a complicated relationship. The series explores themes of family, creativity, and romance in a comedic and often ecchi manner. It aired for 12 episodes from April to June 2017 and has since gained a significant following among anime fans.

7/10

Gochuumon wa Usagi Desu ka?

Gochuumon wa Usagi desu ka? is a cute and heartwarming slice-of-life anime series about a group of girls who work and hang out at various cafes in a small town. The story follows Cocoa Hoto as she begins a new life in the town and makes friends with the other cafe workers, including the talking rabbit, Tippy. The show explores themes of friendship, community, and finding one's place in the world, while also showcasing the charming and colorful world of the cafes.

8/10

Shiroi Suna no Aquatope

Shiroi Suna no Aquatope is a 2021 original anime series produced by P.A. Works. The story follows the encounter between a former idol, who has lost her passion for singing, and a high school girl, who works at an aquarium in Okinawa. Together, they learn about the mysteries and wonders of marine life and work to save the struggling aquarium from closing down. The anime explores themes of self-discovery, friendship, and the importance of preserving the environment.

8/10
